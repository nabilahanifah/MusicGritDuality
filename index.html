About Me

Hi there! Nabila is here. I was born in Bandung on November 29th, 2007. I really love loud music, even though i don`t get too deep into the songs, i really enjoy them, i like every genre of music, since then music just means so much to me. I also play guitar, even though I`m not really good at it, but proud of myself. I also love writing in my diaries, I write some random thoughts, sometime I write song lyrics that resonate with me. But for now I`m trying to learn how to draw people maybe even characters, but since I can`t really draw yet, I pour my creativity into abstract doodles. While I love loud music, I also enjoy peaceful, quiet moments, along with cute and funny things. A balance of contrasting elements that make me who I am.
